How this works & things to remember

▾

Each CEO submits their 18-question assessment to you via email (Formspree). Open the Assessor Portal — it walks you through 5 steps per organisation: log their submission, score the 9 domains (0–5 each), run the validation meeting, agree a problem statement and SMART objectives, then lock the baseline. Once locked, everything flows automatically into the SMART Tracker for programme delivery.

Always score to the lowest evidenced level — not what the CEO claims, but what they can show. Scores can only be adjusted once, post-validation, if new material evidence emerges. Once locked, the baseline is permanent.

Use the same browser each time (for now) — data is currently saved locally in your browser. Export JSON regularly and share the file with each other to stay in sync. We're wiring up Airtable as a shared backend — once live, data will sync automatically and you won't need to export/import.

Issue CEO Feedback within 48 hours of locking — use the CEO Feedback Template above. Include their maturity level, key strengths, constraints, and priority focus areas.

After baseline lock — the SMART Tracker takes over. You're done with the Assessor Portal for that org. The tracker already has their locked scores, problem statement, goals, and SMART objectives (it shares the same data). From June to December, the monthly rhythm is: group session → 1:1 consulting outreach → coaching cycle → log everything in the tracker. The tracker has tabs for each: Profile, Baseline (read-only), Diagnosis, SMART Objectives, 1:1 Consulting (8 outreach cycles), Coaching (up to 21 hours), Endline, and Notes.

Endline in December — the same 9 domains are re-scored using the identical anchors and weightings. The tracker shows the side-by-side comparison against the locked baseline automatically. This is your controlled measure of commercial change across the programme.
