01
A very warm welcome

Congratulations on being selected to join SELECT — the Built to Scale Social Enterprise Commercialisation Programme, delivered in partnership with InterTradeIreland.

SELECT is built by practitioners, for practitioners.

We believe social enterprises play a vital role in shaping a more inclusive and sustainable future across the island of Ireland. To realise that potential, organisations like yours need strong commercial foundations. SELECT is designed to support that shift, from ambition to sustainable, scalable growth.

This guide sets out everything you need to know about the year ahead: what SELECT involves, the support you will receive, what we will ask of you, and the key dates to hold in your diary. Please read it carefully and come to our first session ready to engage fully.

Fiona and Clodagh

02
What is SELECT?
The challengeSocial enterprises have strong impact and potential, but this is often held back by unsustainable pricing, fragile financial systems, unclear market focus, or stretched leadership capacity.
The gapMany programmes focus on training. While useful, training alone rarely leads to the structural change needed to grow traded income sustainably.
What SELECT isA nine-month programme that combines diagnosis, practical consulting, and leadership development, so you are actively building commercial growth, not just learning about it.
How it worksEverything is connected and focused on developing your organisation's Commercial Strategy and Operating Plan.
What you receive1:1 consulting with Fiona or Clodagh · Executive coaching with Sarah or Aoife · Eight group learning sessions · A peer CEO network · A two-day residential
Where it startsThe programme begins with a Commercial Baseline Assessment, giving a clear, evidence-based view of your organisation and identifying priorities for growth.
How it buildsModules introduce key tools and frameworks · Consulting applies them directly to your organisation · Coaching supports you to lead change · Outputs build into your final plan
What you achieveA clear commercial strategy · A defined revenue model · A structured market plan · A robust financial model · An operating plan · Strengthened governance · A 12-month roadmap
Your roleSELECT requires active participation. The organisations that benefit most engage fully, apply learning in real time, and make commercial decisions throughout.
In summarySELECT is not a training programme. It is a structured process to build your organisation's commercial capability and sustainability, from baseline through to an actionable plan.

03
Programme ambition & outcomes

SELECT is designed to deliver real, measurable change within your organisation. By the end, you will not just have participated. You will have built and implemented the foundations of a stronger commercial organisation.

By programme completion, you will have:

  • A clear and actionable Scale & Change Plan, grounded in your market and growth opportunity
  • A more focused and confident commercial leadership approach, with stronger decision-making
  • A defined and sustainable revenue model, supported by appropriate pricing and financial clarity
  • Improved operational systems and processes to support delivery at scale
  • Stronger governance and organisational structures, reducing risk and dependency
  • Increased readiness to pursue investment, partnerships and cross-border opportunities

What success looks like

You are making better commercial decisions, faster. Your organisation is less reliant on uncertain income. You have greater clarity on where and how you will grow. You are equipped to deliver and sustain that growth.

The programme begins on 30 April 2026, with the Commercial Baseline Assessment setting the foundation for all work that follows.

05
What we ask of you

SELECT is a high-engagement programme. To gain the full benefit, and to meet the requirements of InterTradeIreland, we ask for consistent, active participation throughout.

Your commitment

  • Attend all group sessions: attendance at all eight sessions is required
  • Complete your Commercial Baseline Assessment: provide honest responses and submit supporting documentation on time
  • Engage fully in 1:1 consulting: come prepared, contribute actively, follow through on agreed actions
  • Participate in executive leadership coaching: use coaching sessions to reflect, challenge your thinking, and strengthen your leadership
  • Maintain your SMART Tracker: keep your actions, milestones and progress up to date
  • Engage constructively with your peer cohort: share openly and respect the experience of others
  • Communicate early: flag scheduling conflicts as early as possible
The more you put into SELECT, the more your organisation will gain from it.
06
Confidentiality

Confidentiality is central to how SELECT operates. Creating a trusted environment allows for open, honest discussion, which is essential for meaningful progress.

  • Group sessions: what is shared in the room stays in the room
  • Coaching: all conversations are fully confidential
  • Enterprise information: never shared with other participants without your permission

All participants will be asked to formally agree to these principles at Group Session 1.

After Group Session #1, complete your assessment using the links in Section 10 — Your first steps. You can complete it online or download the workbook.

1
Complete your assessment — 30 April – 7 MayFollowing Group Session #1, start your Commercial Baseline Assessment using the link in Section 10. Complete it honestly by Wednesday 7 May.
2
Supporting documents — alongside pre-workGather and send the documents listed below. The more you send, the sharper your validation meeting will be.

09
Programme places, reserves & withdrawals

SELECT has been designed for a cohort of 10 participating enterprises. To protect cohort continuity and the integrity of the programme, two reserve enterprises have also been selected.

If a participating enterprise withdraws before the Baseline is Locked, a reserve may be invited to join. Once Baseline Lock is complete, reserve places will not be activated.

If circumstances change and you have concerns about your ability to participate fully, please speak to the programme team as early as possible.

Your SMART Tracker
Track your progress throughout the programme

Your SMART Tracker is the single place where everything comes together — your baseline scores, commercial problem statement, SMART objectives, consulting cycle progress, coaching sessions, and endline comparison.

After your baseline is locked, your tracker will show your scores, the plan Fiona and Clodagh have built with you, and the actions agreed at each 1:1 consulting session. It updates throughout the programme so you can see exactly where you are.

What you will see

  • Baseline scores — your 0–5 rating across all 9 commercial domains, locked after your validation meeting
  • Problem statement & SMART objectives — the core commercial constraint and the measurable targets agreed for the programme
  • Consulting progress — notes and actions from each 1:1 outreach session with Fiona or Clodagh
  • Coaching progress — themes and development from your executive coaching sessions
  • Endline comparison — in December, your scores will be re-assessed using the same methodology, showing measurable progress

How to access your tracker

After your baseline is locked (by 19 May), Fiona or Clodagh will send you two things:

  • A personal link — a URL unique to your organisation. When you open it, the page will greet you by name.
  • A password — unique to you. Enter it to access your tracker. No one else can see your data.

Bookmark the link and keep the password somewhere safe. You will use it throughout the programme to check your progress after each consulting or coaching session.

Your tracker is read-only. Fiona and Clodagh update it after each session with notes, actions, and progress. If you spot anything that needs correcting, contact them directly.

Frequently asked questions
What if I must miss a group session?
Attendance at all eight sessions is required. If you have a genuine scheduling conflict, contact the programme team as early as possible.
Can I send a delegate to sessions in my place?
No. SELECT is designed for CEOs. The programme is built around your direct participation.
Will missing documentation affect my baseline?
Send what you can. Any gaps will be noted and may limit how precisely we can assess certain domains, but it will not prevent you from completing the baseline.
How much time should I budget each month?
Expect approximately 3–4 days per month. This includes group sessions, 1:1 consulting, coaching and the time you invest implementing actions between modules. The baseline month (April–May) is the heaviest. The CEOs who make the most progress are those who apply learning between sessions, not just during them.
